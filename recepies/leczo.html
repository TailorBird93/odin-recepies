Leczo

Back to main

Description

Leczo is a simple Hungarian dish made of peppers, onions, tomatoes, and smoked sausage.

All is fried in lard and seasoned with powdered paprika.

Leczo is usually eaten with either potatoes or bread

Ingredients:

  • Lard (50g)
  • 2 brown onions
  • Smoked sausage (400g)
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 bell peppers
  • Powdered paprika (2 teaspoons)
  • Spicy powdered paprika (1/2 teaspoon)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tomatoes (1kg)
  • Passata (500ml)

Steps:

  1. Put the lard in a wide pot on medium heat
  2. Dice the onions and add them to the pot
  3. Fry them off for about 5 minutes
  4. Add garlic and fry for additional 2 minutes
  5. Dice the peppers and add them to the pot
  6. Slice the sausages and add them to the pot
  7. Add all of the spices and stir thoroughly
  8. Add the tomatoes (peeled and diced) and let them cook for 2 minutes
  9. Add the passata, stir thoroughly
  10. Turn the heat to low and let simmer for 15 minutes
  11. Serve and enjoy!
